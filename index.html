Leh-Ladakh Trip

"We are a group of three friends who worked in the same office 20 years back. We parted ways due to our jobs but to catch up we decided to have this trip to Leh-Ladakh with our families. It was an experience none of us would ever forget in our lives. It all started when we were on a conference call and one of us saw a travel site advertisement of Leh-Ladakh. He randomly asked,"Let's plan a Ladakh Trip?" In no time, we were planning our schedules for the trip. These sudden plans that we make in life, not thinking twice about our job-leaves,kids exams, I feel these are the little things that keeps us kicking. So now that it's been a year to our trip, I present you the glimpses of our entire trip. "...

Just to to introduce you all to the the characters of our story, we are a group of 3 families one, Papals from aurangabad, Kulkarni from Vadodara and Anantpurkars from mumbai.show the current status as they have left their respective houses and they will be reaching my place anytime soon today. But as we all know nothing happens easily today 10th of June it is raining heavily in Mumbai and suburbs. It is 9:30 and by now papal would have a reached in the mumbai railway station but we got to know that the train is delayed by 3 hours. It is 11:45 in the night my kids have fallen asleep and i just got call from both of them and they said they be reaching in an hour. In the middle of this, i forgot to tell you the flight timings, it is for 4:50 a.m. Tomorrow morning and we are supposed to leave my house at 2:30 a.m. It is already 12 o'clock i think i'll be taking some rest Goodnight ...

Day 1

We decided Mumbai to be our centre spot. We all gathered in Mumbai one day prior and after a whole week of videocalling each other,watching almost every YouTube video on how to pack your bags for Leh-Ladakh,here we were! All set to go! We had a 5 am flight to Srinagar that day. We reached Mumbai Airport around 4 am. It felt like the air was filled with excitement. Absolutely noone was tired or sleepy inspite of having a super tiring day one day before. On the flight, as we were close to the city, those white mountains were ready to welcome us. It's so strange that such moments make you fall in love. Not only with the nature,with this planet, but somewhere with your inner self too. You feel peaceful. And so did I! We reached Srinagar at 7 am. The first thing you experience, being a complete stranger to the city, is INDIA. The airport has tight security and no evil can escape the eye of the officers. Even after leaving the airport, there are 3-4 soldiers in every few kilometres. You will somehow start developing love for the country when you yourself feel this. Also we had already started to feel the cold air out there. We never knew what was coming on our way! We had a very kind man- Faarookh - waiting for us with the car right outside the airport. We decided to head to Kargil and watch few tourist spots on the way. ...
